About the Book

Crypto Cycles Playbook is your no-nonsense guide to understanding how Bitcoin and altcoins move through cycles — and how to use those rhythms to your advantage.

This book breaks down what Bitcoin is, why it was created after the 2008 financial crisis, and how its price movement is tied to the U.S. stock market. You'll learn how altcoins often follow Bitcoin's lead and how to spot these moves early.

Whether you’re brand new to crypto or already active but tired of feeling one step behind, this guide will sharpen your edge.

What You'll Learn

  • Market psychology and why prices move in cycles
  • Understanding Bitcoin dominance and altcoin rotations
  • How to use custom Fibonacci levels (including the secret 70.2%)
  • On-chain metrics, funding rates, and ETF flows decoded
  • Battle-tested risk management strategies
